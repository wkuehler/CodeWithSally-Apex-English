red lentil curry that i make all the time. you need a cup of red lentils rinsed, a can of coconut milk (full fat), a tablespoon of curry powder, a teaspoon each of cumin and turmeric, one onion diced, 3 garlic cloves minced, a 1 inch piece of ginger grated, a can of diced tomatoes, two cups of vegetable broth, a couple cups of baby spinach, salt to taste, and some oil for sauteing.

saute the onion in oil for 4-5 min until soft. add garlic, ginger, and all the spices, cook 1 min till fragrant. dump in the tomatoes, lentils, broth, and coconut milk. bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer uncovered for about 20-25 min until lentils are soft and it thickens up. stir in the spinach right at the end until wilted. season with salt.

serves 4. serve with rice or naan.
