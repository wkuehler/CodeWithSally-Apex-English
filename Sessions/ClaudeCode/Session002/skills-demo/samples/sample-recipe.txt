ok so for this chicken stir fry you need about a pound of chicken breast cut into strips, a couple tablespoons of soy sauce, some sesame oil maybe a teaspoon, a red bell pepper sliced, half an onion, two cloves garlic, a knob of ginger, a cup of broccoli florets, and some cooked rice to serve.

heat the sesame oil in a wok on high. toss in the garlic and ginger and stir for like 30 seconds. add the chicken and cook it until its no longer pink, maybe 5 or 6 min. throw in the onion and peppers, cook another 2-3 min. add broccoli and the soy sauce, stir it all together and cook another couple minutes until the broccoli is bright green. serve over rice.

feeds 3-4 people. takes me about 15 min prep and 15 min cooking.
