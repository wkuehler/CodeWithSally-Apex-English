mango lassi style smoothie but dairy free. blend up one ripe mango (peeled and chopped), a cup of coconut yogurt, half a cup of oat milk, a tablespoon of maple syrup, a pinch of cardamom, and a handful of ice.

just throw everything in a blender and blend till smooth, maybe 30 seconds. pour into glasses and sprinkle a tiny bit more cardamom on top if you want.

makes 2 servings. best served cold right away.
